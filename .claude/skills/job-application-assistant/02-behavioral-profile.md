# Behavioral Profile

## Overview
Juan's self-assessment identifies him as an **Analytical Relationship-Builder** pattern: strong quantitative/analytical rigor combined with a genuine drive to build relationships with senior stakeholders, paired with comfort operating in fast-paced, high-stakes deal environments.

## Core Behavioral Drives

| Drive | Level | Meaning |
|-------|-------|---------|
| Pace/Urgency | High | Comfortable running multiple live deal mandates simultaneously under time pressure |
| Analytical Rigor | High | Strong in financial modelling, valuation, and structured quantitative analysis |
| Collaboration | Medium-High | Works well in team settings (led a 6-person team at The MergerSightGroup); also comfortable operating independently on deal workstreams |
| Relationship-Building | Medium-High | Builds rapport with senior bankers, founders, and cross-institution stakeholders |

## Strongest Behaviors
- **Quantitative rigor:** Strong at financial modelling, valuation, and structured deal analysis
- **Process/tooling improvement:** Proactively uses AI tools (Claude, LLM workflows) to standardise deliverables and cut turnaround time rather than doing repetitive work manually
- **Multi-tasking under pressure:** Comfortable advising on multiple live mandates (sell-side and buy-side) at once

## How You Work Best
- Fast-paced deal environments with live transactions and real stakes
- Collaborative team settings - values working alongside peers and senior bankers rather than in isolation
- Environments that reward using modern tools (AI/LLM workflows) to work smarter, not just harder

## Growth Areas (frame positively in applications)
- **Communication/networking depth:** Still building out the breadth of relationship-building to match analytical strength - framed positively as actively seeking roles with more client-facing exposure to develop this further

## Mapping to Job Posting Language

When a job posting mentions these keywords, it's a **strong behavioral fit**:
- "fast-paced," "live deal experience," "multiple simultaneous mandates"
- "data-driven," "AI-augmented workflows," "modernizing research/modelling processes"
- "collaborative team," "cross-functional," "senior stakeholder exposure"

When a job posting mentions these, flag as **potential friction** (not deal-breaker):
- "purely back-office," "no client contact," "isolated individual contributor" - Juan is energized by relationship-building, so roles with zero client/stakeholder exposure may be a weaker motivational fit

## Management Style Preferences
- Works well with senior bankers/mentors who provide real deal exposure and direct feedback
- Values structured but not micromanaged oversight, given experience simultaneously handling multiple mandates

## Using This in Applications
- **Cover letters:** Weave in the combination of analytical rigor (modelling, valuation) with genuine enthusiasm for client/stakeholder relationships and for using AI tools to modernize deal workflows
- **CV:** Emphasize the AI-augmented tech M&A experience (Claude/LLM workflows), simultaneous mandate management, and cross-institution collaboration (The MergerSightGroup)
- **Interviews:** Use the BlueBull Partners simultaneous-mandate STAR example for "pressure/prioritization" questions; use The MergerSightGroup leadership example for "teamwork/leadership" questions
- **Don't overstate:** Do not claim extensive people-management experience - team leadership so far is limited to The MergerSightGroup (6-person student team), not a formal management role
